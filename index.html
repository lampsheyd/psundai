Step-by-step Mint / Repay / Withdraw

  1. Connect your wallet.
    Use http://localhost, not file://
  2. Switch to PulseChain (chainId 369).
  3. Load token & vault info.
  4. (If using WPLS collateral) Approve the Vault to spend your WPLS.
  5. Deposit WPLS (ERC-20 collateral).
  6. OR deposit native PLS.
  7. Check how much you can mint safely.
    —
  8. Mint pSUNDAI (creates stablecoin debt).
  9. (To repay later) Approve the Vault to spend your pSUNDAI.
  10. Repay pSUNDAI debt.
  11. Withdraw collateral (WPLS) after repaying enough.
  12. OR withdraw native PLS.
  13. Final check.
    Confirm balances & ratios.
